Courses

First Year

  • APS111/112 – Engineering Strategies & Practice: Design, project management, teamwork.
  • APS105 – Computer Fundamentals: C programming basics.
  • APS162/163 – Calculus I & II: Differentiation, integration, applications.
  • MAT188 – Linear Algebra: Matrices, vector spaces, eigenvalues.
  • ECE110 – Electrical Fundamentals: Circuits, Ohm's law, and basics.

Second Year

  • ECE231H1 – Introductory Electronics: Diodes, transistors, op-amps, circuit analysis.
  • ECE241H1 – Digital Systems: Logic design, Verilog, FPGAs, finite state machines.
  • ECE244H1 – Programming Fundamentals: C++, OOP, data structures, debugging.
  • MAT290H1 – Advanced Engineering Mathematics: ODEs, Laplace transforms, complex analysis.
  • MAT291H1 – Mathematical Physics: Multivariable calculus, vector fields, integrals, EM applications.
  • ECE212H1 – Circuit Analysis: AC/DC, frequency response, operational amplifiers.
  • ECE216H1 – Signals and Systems: Linear systems, convolution, Fourier analysis, sampling.
  • ECE221H1 – Electric & Magnetic Fields: Coulomb, Gauss, Maxwell's equations, EM theory.
  • ECE243H1 – Computer Organization: CPU, memory, assembly, I/O, lab projects.
  • ECE295H1 – Hardware Design & Communication: PCB prototyping, CAD tools, teamwork, documentation.

Transcripts available upon request
